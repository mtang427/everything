WELCOME. I AM THE SCRUM MASTER!

Mission Statement

As scrum master, I strive to be the best person to have ever mastered scrum. I will aim to improve communication between my fellow peers and learn how to better express my ideas, and manage them in such a way so that they are easily accessible to both those within our group, and those on the outside who want to see the skill behind such a masterpiece. I will know that I am successful when our to-do list is kept up to date and our final product shows the depth and efforts we put into it. The key to a successful project is utilizing the skillset of every member, for they will create a product that cannot be rivaled by the work on an individual. By combining the individual abilities of all, our talents will bleed into the products, creating something truly spectacular. Most importantly, I will make sure our team enjoys working together.
